Welcome to Team 3 Tea! Where modern flavours meet authentic roots.

About our drinks

At Team 3 Tea we create drinks for a modern community while staying true to the authentic zhenzhu naicha roots. Choose from traditional tea flavours(black, green), or try one of our many modern takes on boba drinks (espresso, strawberry, chocolate, caramel). Want to add a bit more POP to your experience? Ask about our popping boba today!

Team 3 Tea Values

At Team 3 Tea our staff are trained in creating perfectly QQ boba for our many drinks. Each batch of house-made tapioca pearls are tested to ensure they meet our strict chewy, bouncy requirements. Our flavours are naturally produced and our teas are Free Trade compliant. With our Give Back program, we hold quarterly fundraisers for various charities around the CBRM. By choosing Team 3 Tea you are supporting both our local community and the communities we get our product from.
